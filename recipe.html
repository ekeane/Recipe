Ol Fashioned Pancake Receipe

Check off the ingredients as you use them:

    Ingredients - For Pancake

  • Eamonn's Signature Pancake Mix
  • Milk (or water if desired)
  • Salt
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1 table spoon of sugar

    • Ingredients - Optional Toppings

  • Chocolate Chips
  • Banana
  • Nutella
  • Jam (any flavour)
  • Syrup

Instructions

You can check off steps as you go!

For Pancake:

Preheat griddle to 250 C.

Place ingredients in mixing bowl and stir until smooth.

Pour batter onto lightly greased griddle.

Let each pancake cook for roughly 5 minutes each side.

Enjoy your pancakes!
